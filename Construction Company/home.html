WHO WE ARE

Ripana Construction and Designers Ltd. is a reputable construction ad designing company incorporated by the Registrar of Companies in Kenya in 2011. Ripana is also registered and in good standing with the National construction Authority as constructor in NCA- class 5. Our head office is located in Embu Town along Wambora-Street.
At RIPANA Ltd, we have a pool of dedicated engineers, skilled craftsmen and skilled labourers who ensure that we deliver our clients project within the specified timeline and budget. In addition, we are fully equipped with the right construction machines and tools to effectively deliver our client's project.
From our beginning, we have undertaken construction and design projects in the counties of Embu, Kirinyaga, Tharaka Nithi, Nyeri, Nairobi, Isiolo and Nakuru. Our projects in these counties include construction of office blocks, apartments, administration blocks, modern hostels, commercial high-rise buildings, maisonettes, bungalows and religion based structures. Some of our satisfied clients include Universities, High School, private developers as well as faith based organizations.
